iOS 7 started with a desire to take an experience people love and make it better. To make it even simpler, more useful, and more enjoyable — but still feel instantly familiar. With that in mind AppAddict set out to further improve your experience by giving you AppAddict 4 a truly amazing app that looks and feels great!.

A lot of time, focus and effort has gone into how AppAddict looks and performs on the iPad. The app makes great use of the iPad's bigger screen sizes and resolutions. With full Retina support the app look absolutley stunning and performs flawlessly. See more fantastic apps with AppAddict on your iPad!

AppAddict 4 also works on non-jailbroken devices using iSignCloud technologies. Usually to install apps from AppAddict you would need to be jailbroken. Now it is all simply done by iSignCloud via the AppAddict iOS app.
